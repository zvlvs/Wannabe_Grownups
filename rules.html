League Rules

As of League Year 2022

League Dues - $85

Deadlines and Updates

  • Keepers are to be selected by end of day August 28, 2021
  • DRAFT DAY - September 4, 2022 at 6pm. Virtual and In Person
  • League Dues are due by end of week 4. All outstanding balances will result in locked accounts.
  • Due to NFL change, regular season will now be 14 weeks and the Championship Game will be held week 17.
  • See below for trial year rules in effect for 2022.

Scoring

  • Passing: 1 point per 25 yards/ 4 points per TD/ -2 points per INT/ -1 point per sack
  • Rushing: 1 point per 10 yards/ 6 points per TD
  • Receiving: 1 point per 10 yards/ 6points per TD/ .5 points per reception
  • Fumbles: -2 points
  • Kick/ Punt Returns: Individual player gets 1 point per 20 yards/ 6 points per TD
  • 2 Point Conversion: 2 points
  • Kicking: 1 Point per PAT/ 3 points per field goal of 0-39 yards/ 4 points per field goal of 40-49 yards/ 6 points of 50 + yards. -3 for missed field goals and -1 for missed PAT
  • Defense and Special Teams: 1 point per sack/ 2 points per INT: 2 Points per fumble recovered/ 2 points per safety/ 6 Points per TD/ 6 points per kickoff or PR TD
    • 10 point for 0 points allowed
    • 7 points for 1-6 points allowed
    • 4 points for 7-13 points allowed
    • 1 point for 14-20 points allowed
    • 0 points for 21-27 points allowed
    • -1 points for 28-34 points allowed
    • -4 points for 35 +

Trial Year Rules

In effect for 1 season and to be voted on again before next season

  • Add an extra flex (wr/rb/te) and remove one bench spot to keep the headcount the same.

Rules Change Log

  • 2022 - Reverted QB scoring back to original (4 point throwing TD, -2 point INT)
  • 2022 - Changed the waiver wire from inverse ranking to FAAB (Free agent acquisition budget)
  • 2021 - league voted to increase dues. Tied between $75 and $100. Commish decision to split and settle on $85.
  • 2021 - Implement 1 permanent IR spot (non covid related)
  • 2021 - changed scoring format for kickers - Kicking: 1 Point per PAT/ 3 points per field goal of 0-39 yards/ 4 points per field goal of 40-49 yards/ 6 points of 50 + yards. -3 for missed field goals and -1 for missed PAT
  • 2 keepers were added in 2016. Reduced to a 2 year period 2019. Reduced to 1 year in 2020
  • 6 man playoff system was implemented in 2016
  • Trade backs (trade a player with another owner just to trade back the next week) was voted as “not allowed” in 2016 following “Tradegate
  • We added a punishment for quitting on the league mid season in 2016. Removal from league was the outcome.
  • We changed all flex slots to WR/RB/TE format and added 1 more flex slot in 2019.
  • We changed passing TD’s from 4 points to 6 points in 2019.
  • We added a half PPR point system in 2019.
  • Keeper draft system was added in 2019. A keepers draft position is decided by that players position on the fantasy ranks the year prior. E.g. if the player was 27th in the fantasy ranks the year prior, that’s a 3rd round pick lost in this years draft.
  • We added a week 10 trade deadline in 2019.
  • We changed the playoffs from weeks 15-17 to weeks 14-16 (no more week 17) in 2019.
  • Buy in amount was raised to $50 in 2019
  • Added 1 point for every 20 yards for the defense and for the individual player in 2019
  • Changed INT from -2 to -3 for QBs in 2019
  • Implemented a -1 point loss for QBs for sacks in 2019
  • Temporarily added 2 IR slots designated for players with COVID only in 2020. A one week lock is the punishment for using the slots for other designations.
